Merge Rot - Play Free Online

Drop, collide, and merge your way through pure chaos in Merge Rot — a surreal twist on the classic watermelon merge game. Toss bizarre items into a bowl and watch them fuse into increasingly weird, hilarious, and oddly adorable creations. Simple mechanics, endless madness.

Wild Themes

  • Brainrot & Giga — Sleek Italian-inspired vibes
  • Dogs, Cats & Fellas — Pure cuddly chaos
  • Capybaras — Because why not

Each theme reshapes the entire game into a completely different flavor of madness. Pick your favorite, start tossing, and see how far your mutations evolve.

How to Play

  • Mouse — Move your item left and right
  • Click — Drop the item into the bowl
  • Match items — When two identical items collide, they merge into something bigger and stranger
  • Don't overflow — Keep items inside the bowl or it's game over
